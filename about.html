Our Story

Founded on faith, driven by love, and dedicated to the children of Ibadan.

Street Kids Reformation Home was established with a divine burden to rescue and restore children living on the streets of Ibadan. Under the leadership of Victoria Funmilayo Israel, we offer a Christ-centered home where abandoned, orphaned, and vulnerable children receive unconditional love, daily care, education, and spiritual reformation.

"For the grace of God that bringeth salvation hath appeared to all men."

— Titus 2:11

Our vision is not just to feed and shelter, but to raise a generation of transformed young people who know their worth in Christ and can contribute positively to society. We believe every child, regardless of their past, has a God-given purpose waiting to be discovered.

Our Core Values

  • Unconditional Love

    Accepting every child as they are, just as Christ accepts us.

  • Biblical Principles

    Building a foundation on the Word of God for lasting character.

  • Excellence in Education

    Equipping children with the tools they need for a bright future.
