Programs & Impact

Our Work

Providing holistic care to transform the lives of street children.

01

Daily Care & Shelter

Providing a secure home, nutritious meals, clothing, and medical attention. We create an environment where children can be children again, safe from the dangers of the street.

02

Education Support

Every child in our care is enrolled in school. We provide books, uniforms, and after-school tutoring to ensure they excel academically and reach their full potential.

03

Spiritual Mentorship

Our reformation is rooted in faith. Through daily Bible study, prayer, and character-building workshops, we help children build a strong spiritual foundation.

Skills & Empowerment

Beyond basic education, we equip older children with vocational skills to ensure they are self-sufficient as they transition into adulthood.

Computer Literacy

Basic IT skills and digital literacy to prepare them for the modern workforce.

Tailoring & Craft

Creative skills and vocational training in fashion design and local crafts.

Technical Skills

Practical training in various technical trades based on individual interests.
